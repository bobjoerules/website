Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 6, 2022
Last Updated: July 12, 2025

Thank you for using DaBaby Discord Bot.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our bot on Discord.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or user data. Specifically, we do not:

  • Log or save usernames, IDs, or message content
  • Store any server or channel data
  • Track user activity or behavior
  • Use cookies or analytics services

The bot operates entirely within Discord’s platform and does not interact with any external databases or tracking systems.

2. How the Bot Works

The bot processes commands and interactions in real-time within Discord. All data is handled temporarily and is never stored.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect or process user data.

However, your use of Discord is subject to their own policies. Please review Discord’s Privacy Policy.

4. Children’s Privacy

This bot is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has interacted with the bot inappropriately, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the bot after changes means you accept the revised policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, you can reach us at:

  • Email: email@bobjoerules.com

TL;DR: We don’t collect or store anything. Period.
